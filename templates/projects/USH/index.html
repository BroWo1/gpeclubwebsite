Key Facts

  • Duration: August 2, 1990 – February 28, 1991 (6 months and 26 days)
  • Cause: Iraq's invasion and annexation of Kuwait
  • Coalition Forces: 35 nations led by the United States
  • Operation Names: Desert Shield (buildup) and Desert Storm (combat)
  • Coalition Casualties: 292 killed in action
  • Iraqi Casualties: Estimated 25,000-50,000 military deaths
  • Key Leaders: George H.W. Bush (USA), Saddam Hussein (Iraq)
